# DIVAN cinematic shot contract

## Shot 1: garden threshold

- Twilight Persian garden.
- Illuminated geometric arch.
- Cypress silhouette, restrained pomegranate detail, distant warm light.
- Camera begins calm and outside, then glides steadily forward.
- No people, text, logos, signage, or random glyphs.

## Shot 2: reading alcove

- Camera enters a candlelit alcove and settles on a closed Divan.
- Brass candleholder, parchment, subtle tilework, restrained butterfly detail.
- The final second settles into a slow stable drift or fully stable frame.
- The final composition must be reconstructable as the live BookStage.

## Seam rules

- Prefer one continuous clip when quality and mobile size remain acceptable.
- When using multiple clips, the next clip starts from the actual rendered boundary frame.
- Do not mix video models inside one chain unless a failed generation makes it unavoidable and the visual difference is demonstrably acceptable.
- Preserve native resolution, then create dedicated delivery encodes with FFmpeg.
