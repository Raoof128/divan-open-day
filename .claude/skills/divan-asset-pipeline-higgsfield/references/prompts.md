Reuse the style preamble unchanged across related assets.

## Garden poster

A tranquil Persian garden at twilight viewed through an illuminated geometric arch. Cypress silhouettes, restrained pomegranate branches, subtle tile detail, soft distant lantern warmth, a clear visual path drawing inward, central safe composition for mobile, empty reading-safe zones, calm and inviting rather than fantasy spectacle.

## Reading-alcove poster

An intimate candlelit Persian reading alcove. A closed Divan rests at the centre of a refined table, with a brass candleholder, parchment, restrained geometric tile detail and one subtle golden butterfly near but not on the reading area. Match the garden's palette, materials, perspective and light direction. The arrangement must be reproducible as a live web scene.

## Camera motion

One continuous slow cinematic move with no cuts. Begin in the twilight garden and glide steadily forward through the illuminated arch into the reading alcove. Maintain a calm forward velocity, subtle parallax and stable horizon. Approach the closed Divan and settle during the final second into the exact composition required by the live BookStage. No abrupt orbit, no reverse pull, no motion blur at the final handoff, no text or symbols.

## Negative constraints

No people, portraits, modern signage, readable writing, invented script, floating objects, excessive butterflies, fire hazards, magical explosions, neon colour, generic Arabian palace imagery, Ottoman motifs presented as Persian, or architecture unrelated to the approved visual language.
